## What it is

A stupidly simple feed that polls an RSS endpoint every 30 seconds and animates new items in with a slight overshoot bounce. I wrote it on a Saturday, ran it for a week, and then turned it off because the bounce started driving me up the wall.

## Why I'm leaving it here

Because it's exactly the kind of thing that usually lives in a private Codex folder and dies. Part of the point of this site is to let the throwaway work be visible — the small, the half-baked, the experiments I didn't love. If recruiters are going to stumble across this, I want them to see that "builder" means building the stuff nobody asked for too.

No timeline, no posts, no notes. Just this page and a memory of the bounce.
