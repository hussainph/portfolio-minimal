Trust is the bottleneck, not capability. The best AI products build trust ramps, not capability mountains.

Most "agentic" products I've used skip the ramp entirely — they ask for keys on day one, act autonomously on day two, and wonder why nobody trusts the third suggestion they make. The ramp is the product.
