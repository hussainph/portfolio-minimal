Been messing around with structured outputs from Claude and I think the trick isn't constraining the model harder — it's giving it better examples of what "good" looks like. Schemas are necessary but not sufficient.

Feels related to <Ref slug="trust-ramps">trust ramps</Ref> — you don't ask the model to prove its reasoning upfront, you design the *shape* of a good answer and let the model walk into it.
