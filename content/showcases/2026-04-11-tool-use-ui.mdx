Three takes on the tool-use UI for Clawbox. Ended up liking v2 most — the side-rail keeps the context without hijacking the read.

v1 felt jumpy because the layout reflowed every time the model called a tool. v3 felt heavy because a modal stops everything. The side-rail is the least clever of the three and that's why it works.
